## 🌐 About

**♾️ WeOwnNet** is a cooperative ecosystem where people own things together — real estate, businesses, tools, and infrastructure. We believe the future of ownership is cooperative, transparent, and community-driven.

We're building a network of cooperatively-owned brands, tools, and services that serve members — not shareholders.

| Principle | How We Live It |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Cooperative Ownership** | Members own the ecosystem — not investors |
| **FOSS First** | Open-source software wherever possible |
| **Data Sovereignty** | Your data stays yours — never sold |
| **#SpeedToMarket** | Ship today, iterate tomorrow |

## 🤝 Cooperative Model

We follow the [Rochdale Principles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rochdale_Principles) of cooperative ownership:

- **Voluntary and open membership** — anyone aligned with our values can participate
- **Democratic member control** — one member, one vote
- **Member economic participation** — members contribute to and benefit from the cooperative
- **Autonomy and independence** — self-governing, community-controlled
- **Education, training, information** — we teach what we learn
- **Cooperation among cooperatives** — we build together
- **Concern for community** — local impact, global vision
